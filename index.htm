HELLO THERE

Welcome to my little corner of the web. I'm a curious human being with a love for beauty and the infinitely interesting world we all live in. I'm passionate about a somewhat random miscellany of subjects and am currently focused on nothing in particular.

I love to make things and often enjoy overthinking things. I believe form is function, and function in itself can be a beautiful expression of form. Superfluity oft irks me, but it finds itself in many of my favourite creations. UI and UX design fascinates. I love the way that it can flow and bridge into everything that we come into contact with. I like to overthink things like menus, buttons, and how design often is content.

On this website you'll find various information about me, things I've written, photos I've taken, and some of the projects I've done and experiments I've made.
